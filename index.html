Begining of new journey

Kia Ora!

This is my first ever blog and website. As you probably guessed by now, I really like mountain biking. So, I have decided to decorate it with some pictures of it. This blog will be about programming, my personal experiences and journey through the world of programming. I expect it to be fascinating, challenging and very interesting. Coding for me is the skill that everyone should try. You can create something without using physical materials, resources. It is just the terminal and the power of your mind. You can turn numbers, letters and symbols into whatever you want. You can make monitors of millions of people around the world to display what you want. You can express yourself, share your thoughts, pictures and memories. You can do everything you want. Isn't it fascinating?
